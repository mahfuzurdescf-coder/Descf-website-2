Why This Work Matters

Conservation shaped by field reality

Wildlife in Bangladesh faces multiple pressures — habitat change, misinformation, fear-driven persecution, and ecological instability. The challenge is not only biological, but deeply social.

🌿

About DESCF

Who We Are

DESCF is a Bangladesh-based wildlife conservation organisation with roots in awareness, coexistence, and response-oriented conservation. Our work has grown from practical field realities and a commitment to improving how wildlife is understood, communicated, and protected.

We are strongest where ecological understanding meets public action — and where honest, carefully built work matters more than inflated claims.

Mission

Conservation Through Action

To conserve wildlife through awareness, coexistence, field engagement, and evidence-informed conservation practice in Bangladesh.

Vision

A Safer Coexistence

A Bangladesh where wildlife and people coexist more safely, and where conservation practice is strengthened by local knowledge and long-term responsibility.

Approach

Practical & Credible

DESCF works through awareness, communication, field engagement, and learning-oriented conservation practice — grounded in ecological reality.
